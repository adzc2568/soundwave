Remix tutorial

  • step 1

    Choose beats or piano to remix, and choose musical scale you like.

  • step 2

    A row means a period of time, different blocks in the same column means in the same period of time,you can press more blocks to add sound into the audio.

  • step 3

    When audio is playing , drag the button can change the BPM, you can hear the audio become fast / slow.

  • step 4

    Remember to record the audio when the final you've done.

  • step 5

    Finally, download the audio to PC.Also,upload the audio to join our activity or share to social web.
